In general, texting of messages takes pace instantly, but to make the messages to be delivered at a certain time or on some condition is called as Message Scheduling. It mainly helps in sending the messages to the recipients at certain time, because sometimes user may forget about that or the user might be busy with his work. This method can also be helpful in sending messages to the recipients based on their input message automatically. Sending the message based on the recipient message automatically will help a lot to the organizations such as colleges and recruitment departments.
